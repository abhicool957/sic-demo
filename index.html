Welcome to Sarvoday Inter College

Sarvoday Inter College, Raebareli is one of the most prestigious schools in India. Founded as a Public School in 1972 in Raebareli.Sarvoday Inter College, Raebareli is affiliated to the Uttar Pradesh Board (UP Board), which is the largest educational board in the country. It is recognized by the Department of Education.

The current collge building opened in 1980 with two large play areas for the students and an environmental garden which marked the North-west boundary of the school. The school premises provide a modern working environment that is conducive to teaching and learning.

Life at Sarvoday Inter College on a shared commitment to academic excellence, intellectual growth, art, athletics, high standards of ethical awareness, sportsmanship, and community service. The school's traditions and accessibility to a broad curriculum add depth to each student’s life.
